About Us

ConsoleKitchen provides free video gaming systems to families in need. We do this by collecting existing game systems in good working condition from students and familes in our community. We make sure they work well and are fun! Then we connect with people who can use them. If you have a gaming system to donate or know someone who does, please get in touch with us!
