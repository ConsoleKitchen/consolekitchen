About

ConsoleKitchen is an idea that started years ago when a bunch of middle school basketball players were talking about the new gaming consoles they were going to get for the holidays. We began to think... What are they going to do with their old ones? That's when ConsoleKitchen began to take shape. We take old, dusted gaming consoles and make them just like new again.

Why Donate?

On eBay, the current price of a first generation Wii is around $10. Why not make sure it goes to someone who will actually use and enjoy it? With ConsoleKitchen, you don't get your $10, but you provide new experiences for kids who never had the chance to experience new gaming technologies.
